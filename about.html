Background

A Bit About Me

I'm Sean Kiley, an IT and infrastructure engineer currently at Montana Data Company in Cape Town, where I manage enterprise backup environments, implement security tooling, and build internal platforms that connect every part of the business.

I studied Management Information Systems at Stellenbosch University before completing an Honours degree in Informatics — which is a rigorous way of learning how technology and organisations interact, and how to build systems that actually serve people rather than just technically existing. My honours research explored how generative AI is reshaping higher education.

What drives me is the intersection of reliability and intelligence — infrastructure that doesn't need constant babysitting, and systems smart enough to help people do better work. Whether that means a client's data is recoverable after an incident, or an internal AI assistant that gives your team accurate answers in seconds.

I hold 17 professional certifications across IBM, Druva, and cloud platforms, and I'm continuously adding more. Currently studying for the Microsoft MS-102 (M365 Administrator Associate). I don't collect certs for the sake of it — each one represents a system I now know well enough to be accountable for.
